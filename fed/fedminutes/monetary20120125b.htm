January 25, 2012 Federal Reserve Board and Federal Open Market Committee release economic projections from the January 24-25 FOMC meeting For release at 2:00 p.m. EST Share The Federal Reserve Board and the Federal Open Market Committee on Wednesday released the attached table and charts summarizing the economic projections and the target federal funds rate projections made by Federal Reserve Board members and Federal Reserve Bank presidents for the January 24-25 meeting of the Committee. The table will be incorporated into a summary of economic projections released with the minutes of the January 24-25 meeting.
