March 15, 2017 Federal Reserve Board approves action by the Boards of Directors of the Federal Reserve Banks of New York and St. Louis For release at 4:15 p.m. EDT Share The Federal Reserve Board has approved actions on Wednesday by the Boards of Directors of the Federal Reserve Banks of New York and St. Louis, increasing the discount rate (the primary credit rate) at the Banks from 1-1/4 percent to 1-1/2 percent, effective March 16, 2017.
