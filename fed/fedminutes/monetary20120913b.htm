September 13, 2012 Federal Reserve Board and Federal Open Market Committee release economic projections from the September 12-13 FOMC meeting For release at 2:00 p.m. EDT Share The Federal Reserve Board and the Federal Open Market Committee on Thursday released the attached table and charts summarizing the economic projections and the target federal funds rate projections made by Federal Reserve Board members and Federal Reserve Bank presidents for the September 12-13 meeting of the Committee. The table will be incorporated into a summary of economic projections released with the minutes of the September 12-13 meeting. Summaries of economic projections are released on a quarterly schedule.
