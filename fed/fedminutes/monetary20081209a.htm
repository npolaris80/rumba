December 09, 2008 Federal Reserve announces it will conduct two auctions of term credit through its Term Auction Facility (TAF) in January 2009 For immediate release Share The Federal Reserve will conduct two auctions of term credit through its Term Auction Facility (TAF) in January 2009. It will offer $150 billion of 28-day credit in an auction to be held on Monday, January 12, and $150 billion in 84-day credit in an auction to be held on Monday, January 26.
