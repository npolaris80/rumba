June 29, 2017 Federal Reserve Board to publish Monetary Policy Report to the Congress on July 7 For release at 4:45 p.m. EDT Share The Federal Reserve Board will publish the July 2017 Monetary Policy Report to the Congress at 11 a.m. EDT on July 7, in order to allow members of Congress and the public time to review the report in advance of Chair Yellen's scheduled testimony on July 12. Chair Yellen's opening statement will be released at 8:30 a.m. EDT on July 12. For media inquiries, call 202-452-2955.
