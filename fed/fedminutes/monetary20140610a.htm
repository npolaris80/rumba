June 10, 2014 Federal Reserve announces results of offering of seven-day term deposits on June 9 For release at 12:00 p.m. EDT Share On June 9, 2014, the Federal Reserve conducted a fixed-rate offering of term deposits through its Term Deposit Facility. The operation offered seven-day term deposits at an interest rate of 0.26000 percent. Following are the results of the operation: TDF Operation ID: F42 Total Amount Awarded: $77,769,000,000 Number of Participants: 40 The awarded deposits will settle on June 12, 2014, and will mature on June 19, 2014. The interest rate of 0.26000 percent will apply to all awarded deposits. For media inquiries, call 202-452-2955.
