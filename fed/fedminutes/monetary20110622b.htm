June 22, 2011 Federal Reserve Board and FOMC release economic projections from the June 21-22 FOMC meeting For release at 2:00 p.m. EDT Share The Federal Reserve Board and the Federal Open Market Committee on Wednesday released the attached table and charts summarizing the economic projections made by Federal Reserve Board members and Federal Reserve Bank presidents for the June 21-22 meeting of the Committee. The table will be incorporated into a summary of economic projections released with the minutes of the June 21-22 meeting. Summaries of economic projections are released on an approximately quarterly schedule. Attachment (80 KB PDF) For media inquiries, call 202-452-2955.
