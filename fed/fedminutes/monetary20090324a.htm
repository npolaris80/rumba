March 24, 2009 Federal Reserve announces results of auction of $150 billion in 84-day credit held on March 23, 2009 For release at 10:00 a.m. EDT Share On March 23, 2009, the Federal Reserve conducted an auction of $150 billion in 84-day credit through its Term Auction Facility. Following are the results of the auction: Stop-out rate: 0.250 percent Total propositions submitted: $101.642 billion Total propositions accepted: $101.642 billion Bid/cover ratio: 0.68 Number of bidders: 103 The awarded loans will settle on March 26, 2009, and will mature on June 18, 2009. The stop-out rate shown above will apply to all awarded loans. Institutions that submitted winning bids will be contacted by their respective Reserve Banks by 11:30 a.m. EDT on March 24, 2009. Participants have until 12:30 p.m. EDT on March 24, 2009, to inform their local Reserve Bank of any error.
