April 27, 2011 Federal Reserve Board and FOMC release table summarizing economic projections from the April 26-27 FOMC meeting For release at 2:15 p.m. EDT Share The Federal Reserve Board and the Federal Open Market Committee on Wednesday released the attached table summarizing the economic projections made by Federal Reserve Board members and Federal Reserve Bank presidents for the April 26-27 meeting of the Committee. The table will be incorporated into a summary of economic projections released on May 18 with the minutes of the April 26-27 meeting. Summaries of economic projections are released on an approximately quarterly schedule. Attachment
