March 28, 2008 Federal Reserve will offer $50 billion in 28-day credit through its Term Auction Facility on April 7 and April 21, 2008 For release at 12:00 p.m. EDT Share The Federal Reserve will conduct two auctions of 28-day credit through its Term Auction Facility (TAF) in April. It will offer $50 billion in an auction to be held on Monday, April 7 and $50 billion in an auction to be held on Monday, April 21.
