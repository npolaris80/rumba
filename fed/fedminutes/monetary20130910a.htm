September 10, 2013 Federal Reserve announces results of offering of 28-day term deposits on September 9 For release at 12:00 p.m. EDT Share On September 9, 2013, the Federal Reserve conducted a fixed-rate offering of term deposits with full allotment of tenders through its Term Deposit Facility. The operation offered 28-day term deposits at an interest rate of 0.26000 percent. Following are the results of the operation: TDF Operation ID: F32 Total Amount Awarded: $11,662,100,000 Number of Participants: 25 The awarded deposits will settle on September 12, 2013, and will mature on October 10, 2013. The interest rate of 0.26000 percent will apply to all awarded deposits.
