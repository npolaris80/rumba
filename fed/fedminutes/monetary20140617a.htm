June 17, 2014 Federal Reserve announces results of offering of seven-day term deposits on June 16 For release at 12:00 p.m. EDT Share On June 16, 2014, the Federal Reserve conducted a fixed-rate offering of term deposits through its Term Deposit Facility. The operation offered seven-day term deposits at an interest rate of 0.27000 percent. Following are the results of the operation: TDF Operation ID: F43 Total Amount Awarded: $92,689,500,000 Number of Participants: 45 The awarded deposits will settle on June 19, 2014, and will mature on June 26, 2014. The interest rate of 0.27000 percent will apply to all awarded deposits. For media inquiries, call 202-452-2955.
