November 18, 2014 Federal Reserve announces results of offering of six-day term deposits on November 17 For release at 12:00 p.m. EST Share On November 17, 2014, the Federal Reserve conducted a fixed-rate offering of term deposits through its Term Deposit Facility. The operation offered six-day term deposits at an interest rate of 0.28000 percent. Following are the results of the operation: TDF Operation ID: F52 Total Amount Awarded: $316,020,510,000 Number of Participants: 85 The awarded deposits will settle on November 20, 2014, and will mature on November 26, 2014. The interest rate of 0.28000 percent will apply to all awarded deposits. For media inquiries, call 202-452-2955.
