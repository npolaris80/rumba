December 02, 2014 Federal Reserve announces results of offering of seven-day term deposits on December 1 For release at 12:00 p.m. EST Share On December 1, 2014, the Federal Reserve conducted a fixed-rate offering of term deposits through its Term Deposit Facility. The operation offered seven-day term deposits at an interest rate of 0.30000 percent. Following are the results of the operation: TDF Operation ID: F54 Total Amount Awarded: $402,152,500,000 Number of Participants: 97 The awarded deposits will settle on December 4, 2014, and will mature on December 11, 2014. The interest rate of 0.30000 percent will apply to all awarded deposits. For media inquiries, call 202-452-2955.
