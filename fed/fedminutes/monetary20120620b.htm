June 20, 2012 Federal Reserve Board and Federal Open Market Committee release economic projections from the June 19-20 FOMC meeting For release at 2:00 p.m. EDT Share The Federal Reserve Board and the Federal Open Market Committee on Wednesday released the attached table and charts summarizing the economic projections and the target federal funds rate projections made by Federal Reserve Board members and Federal Reserve Bank presidents for the June 19-20 meeting of the Committee. The table will be incorporated into a summary of economic projections released with the minutes of the June 19-20 meeting. Summaries of economic projections are released on an approximately quarterly schedule. Projections materials: PDF | Accessible Figures
