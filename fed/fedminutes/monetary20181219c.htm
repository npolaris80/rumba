December 19, 2018 Federal Reserve Board approves action by the Board of Directors of the Federal Reserve Bank of Kansas City For release at 5:00 p.m. EST Share The Federal Reserve Board has approved action on Wednesday by the Board of Directors of the Federal Reserve Bank of Kansas City, increasing the discount rate (the primary credit rate) at the Bank from 2-3/4 percent to 3 percent, effective December 20, 2018.
