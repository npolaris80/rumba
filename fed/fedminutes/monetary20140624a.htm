June 24, 2014 Federal Reserve announces results of offering of seven-day term deposits on June 23 For release at 12:00 p.m. EDT Share On June 23, 2014, the Federal Reserve conducted a fixed-rate offering of term deposits through its Term Deposit Facility. The operation offered seven-day term deposits at an interest rate of 0.28000 percent. Following are the results of the operation: TDF Operation ID: F44 Total Amount Awarded: $92,420,010,000 Number of Participants: 46 The awarded deposits will settle on June 26, 2014, and will mature on July 3, 2014. The interest rate of 0.28000 percent will apply to all awarded deposits. For media inquiries, call 202-452-2955.
