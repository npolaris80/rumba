November 19, 2013 Federal Reserve announces results of offering of 28-day term deposits on November 18 For release at 12:00 p.m. EST Share On November 18, 2013, the Federal Reserve conducted a fixed-rate offering of term deposits with full allotment of tenders through its Term Deposit Facility. The operation offered 28-day term deposits at an interest rate of 0.26000 percent. Following are the results of the operation: TDF Operation ID: F33 Total Amount Awarded: $13,531,600,000 Number of Participants: 27 The awarded deposits will settle on November 21, 2013, and will mature on December 19, 2013. The interest rate of 0.26000 percent will apply to all awarded deposits.
